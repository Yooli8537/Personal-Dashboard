# Personal Dashboard

A personal Dashboard with all the information you may need to accompany you along the day.

## Nodes

The dashboard is made of many different nodes which can be toggled using the settings icon in the top right.

- Header: Weekday, Date & Time
- Weather: The weather in your area using the **Openweather API**.
- Notes: A little notes section using **TipTap**.
- Quote of the day: An inspirational quote pulled from the `quotes.json` file, chosen randomly by generating a random seed from the date.
- News: Relevant News pulled from the **Newsdata.io API**.
- App Status: Check the status of an app by inputting it's URL.
- GD Level Tracker: View all the levels you're currently progressing on. Requires the [Geometry Dash Bucket List](https://github.com/Yooli8537/geometry-dash-bucket-list).
- Working Graph: Track how long you've worked on a project.
- Habit Tracker: Track your habits and continue your streak.
- Spotify Widget: What's jamming?
- Upcoming Events: Events from the next 7 days pulled from your Google Calendar. Filter through certain colors.
